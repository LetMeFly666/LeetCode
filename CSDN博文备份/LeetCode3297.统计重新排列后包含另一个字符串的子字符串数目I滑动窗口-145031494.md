<h2><a id="LetMeFly3297_I_1"></a>【LetMeFly】3297.统计重新排列后包含另一个字符串的子字符串数目 I：滑动窗口</h2> <br><p>力扣题目链接：<a href="https://leetcode.cn/problems/count-substrings-that-can-be-rearranged-to-contain-a-string-i/"
